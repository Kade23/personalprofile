About Kade

Here Goes Nothing
My real name is actually Adeyemi Aidi but i coined up KADE from a combination of my middle name , or my First name( i never seem to get the understanding in the Nigerian setting) because hearing "Your slack username should be unique" sort of scarred me to be more personal with my alter ego names. I am a 26 year old Banker....Well my birthday is real soon( April 27th) so i don't know if i should still be claiming 26 *Tears of old age*. It's time to discover myself so i'm greatful a group of selfless youths decided to take their time to grow and help strangers and call the program STARTNG.

Still on Me

I hail from A town called IKARE AKOKO in Ondo state. Not much to talk about when it comes to my home town. It's a village to me, even if i wake up tomorrow to see that they have suddenly built skyscrapers there, it is still a village in my books *shrugs* I work and Live in Akure, Ondo State, Well, not because i want to but Adulthood has humbled me thus far. I love love Music, Dancing, Reading and the occasional liquid courage with my close circle of friends make life bearable and actually beautiful. I don't require much. I'm easy to please *winks*...And I LOVE CARTOONS. Yeah. Thank you

...In Conclusion

I met the temptress called Coding in 2016 and since then i have believed that it would be a great injustice if i let something that lights up so much fiery passion and brings me such fulfilment go. Keep in mind, i have been frustrated to the point of not touching my laptop for a whooping 16 hours but it just brings me joy because coding proves that all problems have a solution, and even more than one way to solve it. If i can't solve life, i can at least always debug.
